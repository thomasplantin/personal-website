Information about me

My name is Thomas Plantin and I am a Franco-American who grew up in France and decided to come to the United States at the age of 17 with the goal of thriving in a new environment. I have just completed a master’s degree in Software Engineering & Systems at The University of Texas at Austin. I discovered programming the summer after my high school graduation, when I spent most of my days tinkering on an Arduino, a microcontroller board for electronic enthusiasts. From there, I went on to tackle more software-based projects, such as building web apps from scratch using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and using frameworks like Bootstrap, Node.js and, Express JS. Feel free to reach out to me - I love meeting new people!
